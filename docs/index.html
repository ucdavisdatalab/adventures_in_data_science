Overview

This is the course reader for IST008, Adventures in Data Science: Social Science Edition. The course is designed to provide students with a basic understanding of computing and network architecture, basic programming skills, and an introduction to common methods in Data Science and Digital Humanities. This course reader provides background information that will help you to better understand the concepts that we will discuss in class and to better participate in the hands-on portion of the course.
